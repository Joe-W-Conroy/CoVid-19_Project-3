    Predictions



    We created this is project to explore two facets of CoVid-19. First, how accurately we could build a predict model and second how best to show the what state of the world was like after 8 months of the initial cases of CoVid-19 were documented.

    The RECOVERY and CASES OVER TIME (US) pages are where we used machine learning to make predictions. We took the first months of raw case data from https://api.covidtracking.com/v1/states/daily.json to make our predictions of what case data would look like a few months forward. Then we compared our models' against the actual data and analysized their accuracy.

    CASE DENSITY is a visualization of our data via Tableau. It focuses on main the density of cases around the world and the United States.

    The TABLE OF THE USA is drawing from the API https://api.covidtracking.com/v1/states/daily.json. It looks at the active cases in the US states and its territories.
